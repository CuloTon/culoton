Toncoin experienced a sharp rally following Pavel Durov's announcement that Telegram will assume a direct operational leadership role within The Open Network ecosystem. The cryptocurrency climbed from approximately $1.35 to $1.80, representing roughly 30% gains and elevating TON among the top 20 digital assets by market capitalization.

Durov outlined the shift as the next phase in TON's technical development roadmap, building on recent network improvements. He noted that transaction fees have declined sixfold to near-zero levels, with Telegram now positioned to become the blockchain's primary validator and guiding force. The messaging platform will oversee new infrastructure including an updated ton.org website, enhanced developer tools, and performance upgrades expected within two to three weeks.

The announcement marks a notable escalation beyond previous commercial partnerships between Telegram and TON. Earlier this year, the TON Foundation designated TON as the exclusive blockchain infrastructure for Telegram's Mini App ecosystem, which reaches over 950 million monthly active users. Telegram confirmed it would continue accepting Toncoin exclusively for non-fiat transactions across its platform services, including Telegram Stars, Premium subscriptions, and advertising features.

This development carries historical significance given Telegram's original role in creating the Telegram Open Network. The project was suspended following regulatory action by the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission regarding unregistered token offerings in 2020. Telegram subsequently agreed to return $1.2 billion to investors and paid an $18.5 million penalty to settle the charges. Community developers continued the network under the Open Network brand following Telegram's withdrawal.

Durov's latest statement represents a departure from the partnership model that has characterized Telegram's recent TON engagement. Rather than expanding an existing cooperative relationship, the announcement signals a structural reorganization placing Telegram at the center of network governance and technical direction. At the time of reporting, Toncoin traded at $1.806.

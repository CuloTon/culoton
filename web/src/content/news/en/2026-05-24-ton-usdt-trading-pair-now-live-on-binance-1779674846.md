Binance has activated trading for the TON/USDT pair, allowing users to trade Toncoin directly against USDT on the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange.

The listing marks another step in TON's integration into major exchange infrastructure. Toncoin, the native token of the TON blockchain—originally developed by Telegram—has grown into one of the top 10 cryptocurrencies by market capitalization, and direct stablecoin pairs are a standard measure of ecosystem maturity and liquidity depth.

TON/USDT joins existing pairs like TON/BUSD and TON/BNB on Binance, giving traders multiple on-ramp and off-ramp options without intermediate conversions. The addition of a dedicated stablecoin pair typically reduces slippage for large orders and improves price discovery for retail participants.

The move comes as TON activity has expanded significantly across decentralized finance, gaming, and payment use cases. Binance's support reflects institutional appetite for deeper liquidity in the pair, particularly among traders seeking direct exposure without volatility noise from other denominations.

No specific trading fees, launch bonuses, or promotion details were disclosed in the announcement. The pair joins Binance's existing TON trading options and follows standard listing procedures for major blockchain assets on the exchange.

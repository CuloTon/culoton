Binance, the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by volume, has added TON/USDT to its trading pairs, allowing users to trade Toncoin directly against Tether's USDT stablecoin.

The listing marks another expansion of TON's presence on major centralized exchanges. Direct trading pairs with stablecoins typically reduce friction for retail traders and institutional participants seeking exposure to an asset without routing through intermediate pairs.

TON, the blockchain network developed by Telegram, has steadily grown its exchange infrastructure over the past year. Binance previously listed TON/BUSD and other pairings, and the addition of TON/USDT reflects sustained demand from the platform's user base.

USDT remains the most widely traded stablecoin in cryptocurrency markets, commanding roughly 60% of stablecoin trading volume. Its pairing with TON gives traders a liquid, established benchmark for price discovery.

No announcement regarding trading fees, minimum order sizes, or promotional incentives for the pair was immediately available. Market participants should verify current trading conditions on the Binance platform directly.

The listing comes as TON has maintained a position among the top 10 cryptocurrencies by market capitalization, driven in part by continued development on the blockchain and expanding use cases within the Telegram ecosystem.

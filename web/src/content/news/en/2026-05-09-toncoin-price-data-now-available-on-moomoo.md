Moomoo, a retail investment platform, has integrated Toncoin (TON) price quotes and news into its financial data services. The addition allows users of the platform to track real-time price movements and access market information for the TON token alongside traditional equities and other digital assets.

The move reflects broader industry consolidation around mainstream financial infrastructure. Retail investment platforms have increasingly expanded their cryptocurrency offerings over the past two years, responding to sustained user demand for exposure to digital assets. TON, the native token of the TON blockchain (formerly known as Telegram Open Network), has maintained a presence in the broader crypto ecosystem despite its complex development history.

Moomoo's inclusion of TON data follows similar integrations by other financial information providers and brokers seeking to offer more comprehensive market coverage. The platform now enables users to monitor Toncoin movements alongside equity positions and other holdings in a single interface.

No specific details were provided regarding the scope of Moomoo's TON integration or whether the platform intends to offer direct token trading. Market data availability represents a preliminary step that typically precedes fuller trading capabilities on retail platforms.

TON remains the 14th-largest cryptocurrency by market capitalization, with sustained development activity and ongoing ecosystem expansion. The token's inclusion on additional distribution platforms continues its gradual integration into conventional financial workflows, though adoption remains concentrated among dedicated blockchain participants rather than mainstream retail investors.

Toncoin has extended its recent correction, with technical analysis pointing to sustained bearish momentum across key timeframes. The pullback follows a period of gains earlier this year, and current price action suggests sellers remain in control.

Chart patterns and moving average positions indicate that resistance levels above the current trading range remain challenged. Momentum indicators are aligned with the downside pressure, suggesting that a bounce would face headwinds from longer-term trend resistance.

Traders monitoring the asset are watching for potential support levels that could slow the decline, though a break below key technical floors could open the door to further downside targets. Volume patterns during the recent move lower suggest participation from larger market participants, lending credence to the directional shift.

The broader market environment has also weighed on altcoins in recent weeks, with reduced risk appetite limiting upside potential for assets like Toncoin. Recovery attempts have stalled at intermediate resistance, a pattern that typically precedes renewed weakness when broader sentiment remains cautious.

Analysts following the TON market note that oversold conditions on certain timeframes could eventually prompt a technical bounce, but such moves would likely be viewed as opportunities to add to short positions rather than signals of a sustained reversal. The prevailing bias remains tilted toward lower prices unless major technical levels are reclaimed with conviction.

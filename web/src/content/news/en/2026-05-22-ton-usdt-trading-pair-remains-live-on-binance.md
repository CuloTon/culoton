Binance continues to list and facilitate trading in the TON/USDT currency pair, maintaining the direct route between TON tokens and the USDT stablecoin on its platform.

TON, the native token of the TON blockchain—originally developed by Telegram—has maintained its presence across major exchange venues since achieving broad market adoption. The pairing with USDT remains one of the primary trading routes for price discovery and liquidity.

Binance's continued support for the pair reflects sustained institutional and retail interest in TON as a blockchain asset. The exchange has been a consistent venue for TON trading since the token became widely accessible to international markets.

Trading volume, spreads, and price action on TON/USDT reflect broader market conditions affecting the cryptocurrency sector. The pair's availability on Binance ensures that market participants have direct access to current price feeds and execution without intermediary conversion steps.

No recent changes to the pair's listing status or trading terms have been announced. The continued operation underscores the routine nature of spot trading in major cryptocurrency pairs on leading exchanges.

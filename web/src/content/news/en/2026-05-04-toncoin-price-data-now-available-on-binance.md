Binance has integrated live pricing data for Toncoin (TON) paired against the United Arab Emirates dirham (AED), expanding its support for real-time market information on the token.

The addition includes a dedicated price tracker, market capitalization metrics, and interactive charts—standard tools for monitoring cryptocurrency performance across major trading venues. For users in the Gulf region or those transacting in AED, the pairing offers direct exposure to TON's price movements without requiring intermediate conversions.

The move reflects Binance's continued expansion of regional currency pairs, a strategy designed to serve traders across different geographic markets. TON, the native token of the TON blockchain network, has maintained steady trading volume on major exchanges since its public launch.

No announcement regarding new listing status or trading incentives was disclosed with the data integration. The addition appears to be a routine expansion of Binance's price discovery infrastructure rather than a significant milestone for Toncoin adoption or ecosystem development.

Traders can now monitor TON-AED pricing alongside other major currency pairs on Binance's trading interface.

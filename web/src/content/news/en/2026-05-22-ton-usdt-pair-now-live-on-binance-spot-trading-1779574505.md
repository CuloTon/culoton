Binance has activated trading for the TON/USDT pair on its spot market, marking an expansion of Toncoin's presence on the exchange. The listing allows users to trade Toncoin directly against Tether's stablecoin on Binance's trading platform.

TON, the native token of the Telegram Open Network blockchain, has been listed on Binance since its initial public offering in 2023. The addition of the USDT pair complements existing trading options and provides another avenue for market participants seeking stablecoin exposure to the asset.

The move reflects continued integration of TON trading infrastructure across major centralized exchanges. Binance remains one of the primary venues for TON liquidity and price discovery, alongside other major trading platforms.

Toncoin has built significant adoption within the Telegram ecosystem, with applications and services deployed across the blockchain. The token underpins network operations, validator participation, and smart contract execution on TON's infrastructure.

No trading restrictions or special conditions were indicated for the new pair. Spot trading on Binance typically operates with standard fee structures and 24/7 market availability. The listing itself does not constitute investment advice or endorsement of any kind—it is purely a trading infrastructure addition.

Binance, the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by volume, has added TON/USDT to its active trading pairs. The listing enables direct trading of Toncoin against Tether's USDT stablecoin on the platform.

The pair joins Binance's existing TON trading options, which already include TON/BUSD and other denominations. The addition of USDT pairing provides traders with another liquidity route for Toncoin exposure, given USDT's prevalence across the broader crypto market.

TON, the native token of The Open Network blockchain, has seen sustained interest from institutional and retail participants since its mainnet launch in 2023. The token maintains close ties to Telegram, whose founder Pavel Durov seeded the original TON project before handing it to the community.

Binance's inclusion of additional trading pairs typically signals confidence in an asset's liquidity profile and market demand. For TON holders and traders, the new pair offers another execution venue for position management, particularly for those preferring stablecoin settlement.

No announcement date or specific market impact metrics were provided with the listing.

Binance has listed the TON/USDT trading pair on its spot exchange, allowing users to trade Toncoin directly against Tether's USD stablecoin.

The pairing joins existing TON trading pairs on the platform and reflects growing institutional and retail interest in the Telegram-backed blockchain's native asset. Binance remains one of the largest venues for TON liquidity, and the addition of a dedicated USDT pair may reduce slippage for larger trades and improve price discovery between the two assets.

TON has maintained its position in the top-20 cryptocurrencies by market capitalization, with trading activity spread across multiple exchanges. The USDT pair complements existing pairings and provides traders with another direct on-ramp to the token without intermediate conversions.

Binance did not announce a promotion or special trading conditions for the new pair at launch. Standard spot trading fees apply. The listing is part of the exchange's ongoing expansion of its pair roster as trading volumes and ecosystem maturity evolve.

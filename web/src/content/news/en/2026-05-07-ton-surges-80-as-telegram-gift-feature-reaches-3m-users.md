Toncoin has posted an 80 percent price gain, coinciding with a significant milestone in Telegram's rollout of native cryptocurrency gifting functionality. The feature, which allows users to send TON directly through the messaging platform, has now been activated for approximately 3 million users.

The expansion represents a material step forward in mainstream exposure for the token. Telegram, with over 900 million monthly active users, has positioned itself as a primary on-ramp to the TON ecosystem. The gifting mechanic—allowing peer-to-peer crypto transfers without requiring separate wallet setup or manual address entry—lowers friction for users encountering crypto for the first time.

Telegram has been methodically integrating TON functionality since mid-2024, beginning with simple balance displays and progressing to transaction capabilities. The gifting feature sits at the intersection of user accessibility and organic network growth; each transfer introduces recipients to the token and platform simultaneously.

The price movement follows a broader pattern in which adoption metrics drive investor sentiment in layer-one blockchains. TON has maintained a position in the top ten cryptocurrencies by market capitalization, with liquidity concentrated across major exchanges. Whether the current rally sustains depends partly on continued feature expansion and user retention within Telegram's ecosystem.

Analysts have flagged that Telegram's role as both application layer and distribution channel creates asymmetric incentives for TON development. The messaging platform's control over feature rollout, onboarding flows, and visibility within its UI gives it outsized influence over token velocity and adoption curves.

No official statement from Telegram or the TON Foundation was immediately available regarding the milestone or future expansion timelines.

Binance has added TON/USDT to its trading pairs, marking another step in the integration of Toncoin into major cryptocurrency exchange infrastructure.

The listing enables direct trading between Toncoin and Tether's USDT stablecoin on the world's largest spot trading platform by volume. This pairing complements existing TON trading options and provides users with a straightforward entry and exit route denominated in a widely-used dollar proxy.

Toncoin, the native token of the TON blockchain, has seen steady institutional adoption since its mainnet launch. The Telegram-backed network has attracted developers and projects seeking alternatives to congested Ethereum infrastructure, with particular traction in gaming, payments, and community-based applications.

Binance's continued expansion of TON pairs reflects broader market confidence in the ecosystem's viability. The exchange has progressively deepened its listing and support for TON-based assets as trading volume and user interest have grown.

No announcement was made regarding special trading conditions, promotional periods, or margin availability for the new pair. Standard Binance trading fees and policies apply.

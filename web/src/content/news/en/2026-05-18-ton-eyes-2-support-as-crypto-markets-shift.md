Whether TON holds this support or breaks through will likely depend on broader risk appetite and any TON-specific developments affecting sentiment.

The cryptocurrency market remains sensitive to macro conditions, regulatory headlines, and on-chain activity. Toncoin, as part of the Telegram ecosystem, draws interest from both retail and institutional observers tracking adoption and utility in emerging blockchain applications.

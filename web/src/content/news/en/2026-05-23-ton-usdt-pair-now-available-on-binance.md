Binance has added TON/USDT to its trading pairs, allowing users to trade Toncoin directly against USDT on the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by volume.

The listing marks another step in mainstream adoption for TON, the blockchain developed by Telegram. The pair's availability on Binance—already home to dozens of TON-related tokens and services—lowers friction for traders seeking exposure to the layer-one network.

TON has grown substantially since its mainnet launch, supported by Telegram's 500+ million user base and the platform's ecosystem of decentralized applications. Recent developments include the deployment of Fragment, Telegram's username auction platform, and expanded support for decentralized finance protocols.

Binance's decision to list the pair underscores institutional appetite for TON infrastructure assets. The exchange already hosted TON/BUSD and other denominational pairs; the USDT option provides another route into the token, particularly for traders with USDT reserves.

The listing does not constitute a price endorsement or technical announcement from either party. TON remains volatile like other layer-one tokens, and trading on any exchange carries standard custody and market risks.

No official statement on volume expectations or listing fees was available at press time.

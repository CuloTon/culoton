Toncoin has maintained its recent uptrend, with traders and analysts monitoring whether the asset can decisively close above the $3.00 price level.

The rally reflects broader strength in the TON ecosystem, which has seen sustained interest from institutional and retail participants over recent weeks. Market structure suggests resistance at the round-number threshold, a level often scrutinised by technical analysts as a potential inflection point for momentum continuation.

Breaking above $3.00 would signal further bullish conviction, potentially opening the path to higher resistance zones. Conversely, rejection at this level could trigger profit-taking or consolidation before the next leg higher.

Market depth and order flow around the $3.00 level will be critical in determining whether the move has genuine follow-through or represents a temporary pause in a longer-term rally. Volume dynamics and behaviour of larger holders will likely influence the near-term trajectory.

The timing of any sustained move above $3.00 may also coincide with broader crypto market sentiment, which has shown cyclical patterns of risk-on appetite and periods of consolidation. TON's performance relative to Bitcoin and Ethereum will remain a key indicator for determining whether strength is idiosyncratic or part of a market-wide rally.

For now, the asset remains in an uptrend, with the $3.00 level serving as both a psychological and technical focal point for market participants positioning for the next phase of price discovery.

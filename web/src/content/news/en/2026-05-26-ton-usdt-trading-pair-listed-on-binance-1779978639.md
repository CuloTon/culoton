Binance, the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by trading volume, has made the TON/USDT pair available for trading on its platform. The listing allows users to buy and sell TON, the native token of the TON blockchain network, directly against the US dollar-pegged stablecoin USDT.

The pair's availability on Binance represents a significant distribution channel for TON, which is the blockchain developed by the Telegram team and launched on the open network in 2023. The exchange has become one of the primary venues for spot trading in major cryptocurrencies and blockchain tokens globally.

TON has experienced considerable activity since its mainnet launch, with the network processing transactions and supporting decentralized applications built on its infrastructure. The token's liquidity on major exchanges like Binance typically reflects institutional and retail interest in the ecosystem.

Binance regularly expands its trading pairs to accommodate demand and provide users with direct conversion options between assets. The TON/USDT pair complements existing trading routes and offers traders a stablecoin onramp for position management.

As of recent market data, TON trades in the broader cryptocurrency market alongside other large-cap blockchain assets. The exact volume and price activity on the pair would depend on real-time market conditions and user trading behavior on the exchange.

Binance maintains active trading for the TON/USDT pair, providing market participants with a direct channel to exchange Toncoin against Tether's stablecoin.

The pair represents one of several TON trading routes available on the exchange. Binance, as the world's largest spot cryptocurrency exchange by volume, has continued to support TON trading since the token's broader market integration in 2023.

TON/USDT pricing and volume reflect broader market conditions for the blockchain token, which is native to The Open Network—the Telegram-affiliated Layer 1 blockchain. The pair's availability underscores sustained institutional and retail demand for TON exposure.

For traders and investors monitoring TON's price movement, the USDT pair offers a stablecoin-denominated reference point, commonly used as a base pair for technical analysis and fiat-equivalent valuation across the TON ecosystem.

Binance's continued listing of TON/USDT aligns with the exchange's strategy of supporting tokens with established developer communities and use cases. The trading pair's persistence suggests adequate liquidity and market interest to justify listing maintenance.

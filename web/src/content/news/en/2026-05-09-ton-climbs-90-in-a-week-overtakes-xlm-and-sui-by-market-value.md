TON has posted a significant rally over the past week, gaining roughly 90% in value and climbing the rankings of cryptocurrencies by market capitalization. The surge was enough for the token to overtake both Stellar (XLM) and Sui (SUI), two established layer-one blockchain projects with their own substantial user bases and development ecosystems.

The move reflects broader momentum in the TON ecosystem, which has benefited from continued development activity, growing integration with the Telegram messaging platform—which created the blockchain—and increased developer interest in the chain's tooling and dApp infrastructure. Market movements of this scale are not uncommon in cryptocurrency, where liquidity and sentiment shifts can drive rapid repricing of assets.

TON's ascent in the market-cap rankings signals renewed investor appetite for tokens tied to large-scale communication platforms and their native blockchains. The overtaking of XLM and SUI suggests that capital was rotating into TON from those positions, a pattern typical during periods of heightened trading activity and risk-on sentiment in crypto markets.

The token has maintained attention from both retail participants and institutional observers tracking the Telegram ecosystem and its blockchain layer. Whether the gains prove durable or represent a temporary volatility spike remains an open question; crypto asset valuations often mean-revert following sharp rallies, depending on underlying fundamentals and sustained user adoption.

MEXC, one of the major global spot trading platforms, reported the figures. Trading volume on the exchange and others likely spiked during the rally, consistent with patterns observed during rapid repricing events in crypto markets.

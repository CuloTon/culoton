Shiba Inu, by contrast, has encountered significant selling volume. The memecoin faces resistance as broader market conditions weigh on sentiment, and technical indicators suggest downside pressure persists. The divergence between SHIB's recent weakness and HYPE's strength underscores how fragmented current market participation has become.

Toncoin's return to the $2 level comes after earlier volatility. The asset remains among the larger-cap holdings in the blockchain ecosystem, and price stability at round numbers often serves as a testing ground for institutional and retail conviction. The recovery suggests some stabilization after recent drawdowns, though sustained momentum above this level remains to be established.

Market-wide conditions continue to shift. Major movements in Bitcoin and Ethereum remain the gravitational force for smaller assets, and Toncoin's path will likely track broader macro sentiment more closely than individual catalyst strength. For traders, the current setup presents both support at $2 and potential resistance overhead that merit monitoring in coming sessions.

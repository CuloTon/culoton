Toncoin was trading in the vicinity of $1.954 per token against Tether (USDT) on Binance's spot market, according to current exchange data.

The price point reflects the ongoing market valuation of the TON blockchain's native asset as it trades on one of the world's largest cryptocurrency exchanges. Binance spot trading allows direct purchase and sale of TON for stablecoin settlement without leverage or derivatives mechanics.

TON, the native token of the blockchain built initially by the Telegram team and later developed by a decentralized community, has maintained a position among the larger-cap layer-1 tokens. Trading volume and price discovery continue across multiple venues, with Binance serving as a primary liquidity pool for USDT-denominated TON trades.

The current price level sits within ranges that have characterized the token's recent market activity, though broader cryptocurrency market conditions and on-chain ecosystem developments remain the primary drivers of sustained price movement. Participants in spot markets can execute orders at market rates with immediate settlement, distinguishing this from leveraged or perpetual contract trading.

Binance's spot markets for TON pairs continue to serve as a reference point for institutional and retail traders managing TON exposure through stablecoin pairs.

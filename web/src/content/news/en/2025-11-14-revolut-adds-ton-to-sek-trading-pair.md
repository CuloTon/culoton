Revolut has introduced a trading pair allowing users to convert Toncoin (TON) directly to Swedish Kronor (SEK), broadening the fintech app's cryptocurrency trading capabilities.

The move reflects growing integration of TON, the native token of the TON blockchain network, into mainstream retail trading platforms. Revolut, which operates across Europe and other regions, has steadily expanded its cryptocurrency offerings to meet user demand for alternative digital assets.

By offering TON-SEK conversion, Revolut gives Swedish users and others trading in kronor a direct on-ramp to Toncoin without requiring intermediate currency steps. This type of direct pair reduces friction and appeals to traders seeking efficient access to tokens with growing ecosystem activity.

TON has gained traction among retail users and developers since the TON blockchain became a focal point for decentralized applications and services. The token's market presence extends across multiple trading venues, though concentrated liquidity on specialized crypto exchanges remains higher than on mainstream fintech platforms.

Revolut's addition of the TON-SEK pair is consistent with its strategy of adding emerging blockchain tokens to its platform. The decision reflects broader market trends toward institutional and retail accessibility for tokens beyond Bitcoin and Ethereum.

The platform has not announced volume targets or promotional terms related to the new pair. Trading fees and spreads remain subject to Revolut's standard cryptocurrency trading terms.

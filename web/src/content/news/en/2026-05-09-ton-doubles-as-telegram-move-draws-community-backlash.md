TON, the native token of the TON blockchain, has doubled in value following an exclusive action by Telegram, though the surge has sparked debate within the cryptocurrency community.

Telegram's move—details of which remain sparse in available reports—appears to have catalyzed significant buying pressure. The token's rapid ascent from its previous levels marks one of the more dramatic single swings for TON in recent months.

However, the price rally has not been universally celebrated. Critics within the crypto space have voiced concerns about the nature and implications of Telegram's initiative, raising questions about centralization risks, fairness, or market manipulation dynamics. The exact nature of these objections has not been uniformly reported, but sentiment in community forums suggests unease about how the move may affect TON's long-term positioning as a decentralized asset.

Telegram, which backed the TON blockchain's development and maintains significant influence over its ecosystem, has a track record of making unilateral decisions that move markets. The company's 500 million-plus user base gives it outsized power over token adoption and price discovery.

The price movement has been visible across major exchanges, including MEXC, where the token saw substantial volume increases alongside the advance. Whether the gain holds or contracts will likely depend on broader market conditions and any follow-up announcements from Telegram or the TON foundation.

As with all rapid asset price movements, participants are advised to consider volatility and their own risk tolerance. The underlying technical and community fundamentals of TON remain separate questions from short-term price action.

Binance has activated trading for TON paired against USDT, the stablecoin pegged to the US dollar. The listing marks another expansion of TON's presence on the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by trading volume.

TON, the native asset of the blockchain developed by Telegram, has gained institutional traction since its mainnet launch. The token powers transaction fees, staking, and governance functions across the TON ecosystem, which includes decentralized finance applications, NFT platforms, and payment infrastructure.

The USDT pairing provides traders with a direct route between TON and dollar-denominated value without routing through intermediary pairs. This structure typically increases liquidity and reduces slippage for traders moving between the assets at scale.

Binance's support for TON pairs has grown incrementally. The exchange previously listed TON/BUSD and TON/FIAT pairs, reflecting demand from its user base for exposure to the Telegram ecosystem. USDT remains the most widely used stablecoin across crypto markets and serves as the default trading rail for most blockchain assets on major exchanges.

TON's market position has strengthened as Telegram's messaging platform—which serves over 900 million users—continues integrating blockchain features. The token has attracted venture capital funding and institutional interest, though it remains subject to the same regulatory scrutiny facing other blockchain projects.

Binance has activated trading for TON/USDT, adding another direct exchange route for Toncoin against Tether's stablecoin on the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by volume.

The listing expands the trading infrastructure available to TON holders and those seeking exposure to the Telegram-backed blockchain token. TON/USDT joins existing TON pairs on the platform, offering traders a stablecoin-denominated option alongside other quoted currencies.

The move reflects the growing institutional and retail interest in TON following its emergence as a major blockchain ecosystem. The token has attracted significant developer activity and user adoption, particularly within Telegram's own integration roadmap.

Binance remains the primary venue for TON liquidity and price discovery, and the addition of this trading pair underscores the exchange's responsiveness to ecosystem development. The pairing with USDT—the most-traded stablecoin globally—typically ensures competitive spreads and high volume.

No specific trading conditions or launch details were announced beyond the pair's availability.

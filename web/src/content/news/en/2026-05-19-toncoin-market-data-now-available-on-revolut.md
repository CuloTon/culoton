Revolut, the London-based fintech platform, has integrated Toncoin price data and live charting tools into its application, allowing users to monitor TON's market performance in real time.

The addition reflects growing mainstream adoption of the TON token, which is built on the blockchain developed by Telegram. Revolut's decision to list TON alongside established cryptocurrencies signals the token's increased legitimacy among retail financial service providers.

Users of the platform can now access current pricing information, historical price charts, and market data for Toncoin without leaving the Revolut app. The feature is part of Revolut's broader cryptocurrency offering, which has expanded considerably over recent years to include dozens of digital assets.

TON has emerged as one of the more actively developed blockchain ecosystems, with a growing number of decentralized applications and services built on top of its infrastructure. The token's integration into mainstream financial applications like Revolut typically accompanies periods of increased user interest and market activity.

Revolut's cryptocurrency features are available to users in most European markets and select jurisdictions beyond Europe, subject to local regulatory requirements. The platform has previously faced scrutiny from regulators in various countries regarding its crypto offerings, but continues to expand its digital asset services.

Binance has activated trading for TON against USDT, the stablecoin pegged to the US dollar. The pair joins Binance's existing TON listings and provides traders with a direct route between Toncoin and one of the most widely used dollar proxies in crypto markets.

The addition expands liquidity pathways for TON holders and those seeking exposure to the Telegram-backed blockchain. USDT pairs are a standard entry and exit point for major assets across centralized exchanges, particularly for users managing exposure across multiple positions.

Binance had previously listed other TON pairs, but the USDT pairing offers the advantage of stablecoin settlement—relevant for traders who want to move in and out of position without converting to fiat. The exchange continues to be the primary on-ramp for institutional and retail participation in the TON ecosystem.

No other details were provided regarding trading fees, promotional periods, or margin availability for the pair. The listing reflects ongoing market demand for TON trading infrastructure as adoption across Telegram's user base expands.

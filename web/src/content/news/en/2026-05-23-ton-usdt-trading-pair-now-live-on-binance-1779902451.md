Binance has activated trading for TON against USDT, adding the cryptocurrency to its stablecoin trading pairs. The listing enables traders to move between Toncoin and the dollar-pegged stablecoin without routing through alternative assets.

TON, the blockchain underpinning Telegram's wallet and payment infrastructure, has grown into a material ecosystem over the past year. The addition of a USDT pair on the world's largest spot exchange by volume removes friction for institutional and retail traders seeking direct exposure to the network.

The move reflects broader momentum in TON adoption. Telegram itself, with over 900 million monthly active users, has embedded TON wallet functionality natively into the app, though uptake remains modest relative to the user base. Ecosystem activity has expanded to include decentralized finance protocols, NFT marketplaces, and gaming applications, though most remain small by crypto standards.

Binance has been a selective but consistent supporter of TON listing initiatives. The pair joins existing TON pairs against Bitcoin and other major assets on the exchange.

No official announcement from Binance or Telegram's blockchain division has been issued as of publication. The listing appears to have rolled out incrementally across regional instances of the platform.

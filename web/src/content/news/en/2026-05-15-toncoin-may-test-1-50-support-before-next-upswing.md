Toncoin may face a dip toward the $1.50 level before entering its next significant rally phase, according to technical analysis circulating in crypto markets.

The assessment suggests that TON's recent price action has created conditions where a retest of lower support could occur. Analysts point to chart patterns and key price zones to support this view. The $1.50 mark represents a level of historical significance for the asset, where previous support and resistance dynamics have converged.

The reasoning hinges on typical market cycles: a pullback to established support levels often precedes sustained upward movements. Such consolidation periods allow for fresh accumulation before momentum builds. Market participants watch these technical inflection points closely to time entry and exit positions.

TON's price trajectory remains anchored to broader crypto sentiment and on-chain activity within the TON blockchain ecosystem. The asset has shown sensitivity to developments in the Telegram community and adoption metrics. Any significant news regarding platform upgrades, institutional interest, or ecosystem expansion could alter the technical picture.

While price predictions carry inherent uncertainty, the technical framework being discussed offers traders a potential roadmap: expect downside risk toward $1.50, then watch for reversal signals that might confirm the start of a rally phase. The strength of any bounce from that level would be critical in determining whether the predicted advance materializes.

Analysts stress that stop-losses and risk management remain essential, as markets frequently surprise even well-reasoned technical calls. The key variable will be whether TON maintains broader support in the crypto market or faces headwinds from macro conditions.

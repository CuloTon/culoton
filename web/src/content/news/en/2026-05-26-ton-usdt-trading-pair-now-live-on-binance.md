Binance, the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by trading volume, has activated trading for TON/USDT — a direct pairing between the TON blockchain's native token and the USDT stablecoin.

The listing marks another step in TON's integration across major trading infrastructure. Binance users can now execute spot trades between TON and USDT without intermediary conversions, a feature that typically reduces slippage and improves execution for market participants moving capital in and out of the asset.

TON, the blockchain and token launched from the Telegram ecosystem, has grown to become one of the top 10 cryptocurrencies by market capitalization. The token powers validation, governance, and transaction fees on the TON network, which has attracted significant developer activity and ecosystem growth over the past year.

Direct pairings with major stablecoins are a standard measure of exchange commitment and typically reflect confidence in an asset's trading demand. The addition to Binance's spot trading menu follows similar listings on other tier-one platforms and signals continued mainstream adoption pathways for TON.

No additional details regarding trading fees, minimum order sizes, or promotional terms were disclosed in the announcement.

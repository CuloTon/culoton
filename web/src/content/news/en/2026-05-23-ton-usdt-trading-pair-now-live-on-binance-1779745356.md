Binance has activated trading for TON against USDT, the TRON-based stablecoin, marking another step in the ecosystem's integration with major centralized venues.

The listing allows traders to move directly between Toncoin and USDT without routing through intermediate pairs. For Binance users, the move simplifies execution on one of the most actively traded crypto assets and widens the on-ramp options for TON denomination.

Toncoin, the native token of The Open Network blockchain backed by Telegram founder Pavel Durov, has grown in market presence over the past year. The listing follows earlier integrations across Binance's spot, futures, and staking products.

Direct USDT pairs are typically prioritized by exchanges where trading volume and user demand warrant the operational cost. The addition suggests sustained institutional and retail interest in TON holdings and position management against stablecoins.

No specific trading fees or launch bonuses were announced in the initial listing notice. Binance has not disclosed unique incentive structures for the pair.

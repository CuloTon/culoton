Binance, the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by trading volume, has made the TON/USDT pair available for trading on its platform.

The listing marks another step in TON's integration across major centralized exchanges. TON, the native token of the Telegram-backed TON blockchain, had previously been available on Binance against other trading pairs, but the direct USDT pairing simplifies entry and exit for traders using the dollar-pegged stablecoin.

The move reflects ongoing institutional support for the TON ecosystem. Since its mainnet launch in 2023 following Telegram's acquisition of the blockchain project, TON has established itself as one of the top 20 cryptocurrencies by market capitalization. Telegram's 700-plus million user base has positioned the network as a potential gateway for mainstream adoption.

For traders, a dedicated TON/USDT pair on Binance reduces the need for multi-leg trading routes and typically narrows bid-ask spreads, lowering friction costs. The pair joins existing TON trading options against Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other assets on the exchange.

The listing occurs amid broader expansion of TON's presence across trading venues and DeFi platforms. The blockchain continues to attract developer activity and native projects, though it remains significantly smaller in TVL than Ethereum or Solana.

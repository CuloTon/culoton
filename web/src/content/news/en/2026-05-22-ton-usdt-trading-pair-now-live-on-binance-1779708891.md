Binance has added TON/USDT to its spot trading offerings, allowing users to trade Toncoin directly against the stablecoin without intermediary conversions. The listing marks a straightforward expansion of the exchange's TON coverage, which already included other trading pairs across the platform.

Direct USDT pairings are standard practice on major exchanges; they reduce slippage for larger orders and simplify the trading experience for users who want to move between TON and dollar-pegged value without routing through Bitcoin or Ethereum intermediaries.

TON, the native asset of the TON blockchain originally developed by Telegram, has seen steady adoption since the network's revival under community stewardship following Telegram's exit. Binance has been a primary venue for TON trading since the asset's public launch, and the addition of a USDT pair reflects ongoing institutional appetite for the ecosystem.

No specific trading metrics, volume targets, or launch date have been provided. The pair joins an established set of TON trading options across Binance's platform, catering to both retail and institutional traders seeking efficient exposure to the asset.

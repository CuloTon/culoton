Binance has introduced trading for TON against USDT, the leading USD-pegged stablecoin, marking another institutional trading gateway for the token underpinning Telegram's blockchain ecosystem.

The addition of the TON/USDT pair follows months of expanding infrastructure around TON, which launched as The Open Network on Telegram's Layer 1 blockchain. The pairing allows traders to move directly between TON and dollar-denominated value without requiring intermediate conversions, a feature typically reserved for assets with meaningful trading volume and institutional interest.

Binance remains the dominant venue for TON trading and has been the primary on-ramp for the token since its public availability expanded. The exchange currently hosts multiple TON pairs, including TON/BUSD and TON/BNB. The addition of USDT—Tether's stablecoin, which accounts for the bulk of dollar-denominated crypto trading—further cements Binance's position as the primary price discovery mechanism for TON.

Telegram has built TON as a settlement and utility layer designed to handle high transaction throughput, with wallet integration directly into the app for over 900 million users. The token itself powers transaction fees, smart contract execution, and serves as collateral in various dApps built on the network.

The move reflects broader exchange strategy to support emerging Layer 1 chains with sufficient liquidity and user bases. Trading pair expansion often precedes or accompanies periods of user growth and developer activity on the underlying network.

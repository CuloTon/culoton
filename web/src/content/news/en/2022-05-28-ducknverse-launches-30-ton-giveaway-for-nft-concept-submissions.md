Ducknverse, a project building on the TON blockchain, has announced a giveaway program offering 30 TON in total prizes for community members who submit ideas for their gooz NFT collection.

The initiative appears designed to crowdsource creative direction for the project's NFT development while distributing tokens to engaged community participants. By tapping into community input, Ducknverse is inviting holders and interested parties to propose features, designs, or concepts that could shape the evolution of the gooz series.

Giveaways tied to creative submissions have become a common engagement mechanism in the TON ecosystem, particularly among NFT and gaming projects seeking to build active communities around their collections. The structure—offering meaningful token incentives for substantive contributions—aligns with broader community-driven development trends in blockchain projects.

The 30 TON prize pool represents a modest but meaningful allocation for the submission phase, priced to reward genuine contributions without inflating expectations. For context, such giveaways typically operate on a first-come, best-reviewed basis, with winners selected by project moderators or through community voting.

Ducknverse's approach reflects the ongoing tension in NFT projects between maintaining development control and empowering the community with a genuine voice in creative decisions. Whether the giveaway generates substantive ideas or primarily serves as a promotional tool remains to be seen, but the mechanism itself underscores the market's continued interest in participatory project governance.

Binance has activated trading for the TON/USDT pair on its spot market, adding to the exchange's existing TON trading options. The listing extends access to Toncoin for users seeking to trade the token denominated in USD Tether.

TON, the native token of the TON blockchain—built originally by Telegram before community takeover—has grown as a significant layer-1 network with applications in payments, DeFi, and gaming. Binance's support for additional trading pairs reflects institutional confidence in the ecosystem.

The move comes as TON has consolidated its position among major blockchain networks. The token has seen activity from retail and institutional participants, with exchange listings typically serving as a barometer of mainstream adoption interest.

No specific details were provided regarding trading conditions, fee structures, or promotional incentives surrounding the launch. Binance has not yet issued a formal announcement beyond the listing itself.

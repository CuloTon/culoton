Toncoin experienced a significant single-day rally of 36%, coinciding with news that Telegram has taken control of the TON blockchain.

The messaging platform's decision to assume operational stewardship of the network marks a substantial shift in the protocol's governance structure. Previously, the TON chain operated under a more distributed model, but Telegram's move centralizes key decision-making authority within the company.

The price movement reflects market sentiment around this transition. Some investors view Telegram's direct involvement as a positive development, given the platform's established user base of hundreds of millions and its resources to drive ecosystem growth. The company's backing could accelerate adoption and legitimacy for both the blockchain and native token.

Others in the crypto community have raised questions about the implications of centralized control over what was initially conceived as a decentralized network. The shift raises philosophical and practical concerns about governance, censorship resistance, and long-term decentralization prospects.

Telegram has been deeply involved with TON since its inception, but the formal assumption of control represents a change in operational structure. The company has historically invested significant effort in developing blockchain infrastructure and exploring cryptocurrency integrations with its core messaging platform.

The 36% rally suggests market participants are pricing in optimism about Telegram's ability to execute on the network's roadmap and expand the user base through the messaging app's existing distribution channels. Whether this momentum sustains will depend on the company's delivery on technical milestones and ecosystem development initiatives in the coming months.

TON has competed for developer and user attention within a crowded landscape of layer-one blockchains. Telegram's resources and reach could prove decisive in differentiation, though increased centralization may create friction with decentralization-focused segments of the crypto market.

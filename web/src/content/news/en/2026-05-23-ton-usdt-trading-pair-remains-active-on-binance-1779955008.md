Binance lists TON against USDT as an active trading pair on its spot exchange platform. The pairing represents one of the primary on-ramp routes for TON token holders seeking to convert holdings into the stablecoin or vice versa.

TON, the native blockchain asset of the TON network originally developed by Telegram, has maintained consistent exchange listings across major cryptocurrency venues. The TON/USDT pair on Binance serves both retail and institutional traders managing exposure to the token.

The pair's availability underscores the continued operational integration of TON into mainstream cryptocurrency trading infrastructure. Binance, as the world's largest centralized exchange by volume, plays a material role in price discovery and liquidity provisioning for the asset.

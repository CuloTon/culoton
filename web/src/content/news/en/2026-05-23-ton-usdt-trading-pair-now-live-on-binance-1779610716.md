Binance has activated trading for TON against USDT, the dollar-pegged stablecoin. The listing marks another step in mainstreaming access to Toncoin, the native asset of the TON blockchain developed by Telegram.

The pairing allows traders to move directly between the stablecoin and TON without routing through intermediary assets. Binance remains one of the largest venues for TON liquidity, and the addition of a USDT pair reflects sustained institutional appetite for the token.

TON has emerged as one of the more established Layer 1 networks outside the Ethereum ecosystem, with particular strength in the Commonwealth of Independent States and Eastern European markets. Telegram's ownership and the TON blockchain's integration with the messaging platform have positioned it as a vehicle for mainstream adoption in regions with high Telegram penetration.

The move comes as major exchanges continue to expand their stablecoin trading infrastructure. USDT pairs typically offer tighter spreads and lower slippage than routing through BTC or ETH, making them preferred entry and exit points for price-sensitive traders.

No statement was immediately available regarding trading volume expectations or promotional incentives accompanying the listing. Market data will clarify whether the pair attracts meaningful volume relative to existing TON trading routes.

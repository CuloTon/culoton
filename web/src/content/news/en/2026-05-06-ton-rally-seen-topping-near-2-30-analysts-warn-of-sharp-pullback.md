Trading analysts monitoring TON's recent momentum have flagged what they view as unsustainable price levels, with forecasts pointing to a near-term peak followed by a significant drawdown.

According to technical assessments circulated by MEXC, TON's rally has reached overbought territory on major timeframe indicators. The analysis targets $2.30 as resistance where momentum may exhaust, setting the stage for a reversal. A 30% correction from that level would place the token around $1.60.

Overbought conditions typically indicate that prices have risen too quickly relative to underlying trading volume and momentum, creating vulnerability to profit-taking and liquidations. While such readings do not guarantee immediate reversals, they suggest traders should exercise caution at elevated levels.

The specific price targets reflect a working hypothesis based on chart patterns and oscillator readings rather than fundamental reassessment of TON's utility or ecosystem. Market participants should note that technical forecasts carry inherent uncertainty, particularly in volatile cryptocurrency markets where sentiment shifts can override pattern-based predictions.

TON's price movements have attracted increased attention as the token consolidates gains from earlier in the year. The project continues development across its blockchain infrastructure and ecosystem applications, independent of short-term trading cycles.

Traders holding positions near resistance levels may want to review their risk management strategies. Those considering entries should weigh the projected correction scenario against their own analysis and timeframe.

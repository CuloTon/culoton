Governance votes on TON are conducted through the blockchain's on-chain mechanisms, where token holders and delegated validators participate in decisions affecting protocol parameters and treasury allocations. The scale of this particular proposal—affecting 20% of total supply—underscores its significance for the broader ecosystem.

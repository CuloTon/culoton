Binance, the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by volume, has added TON/USDT to its trading pairs, allowing users to trade Toncoin directly against Tether's USD stablecoin.

The listing expands TON's accessibility on one of crypto's most heavily trafficked platforms. TON is the native blockchain token of The Open Network, originally developed by Telegram before becoming a community-driven project. The token powers transaction fees, staking, and governance on the network.

TON/USDT availability on Binance simplifies entry and exit for traders and investors without requiring intermediate conversion steps. The pair joins other major TON trading venues already established across the broader exchange ecosystem.

The move reflects Binance's continued support for ecosystem tokens outside the major layer-one blockchains. TON has garnered institutional interest in recent months, with the broader ecosystem expanding its developer base and on-chain activity.

No specific details on trading fees, minimum order sizes, or promotional terms were disclosed by Binance in publicly available announcements. Users can access the pair through Binance's spot trading interface.

Binance has activated trading for the TON/USDT pair, allowing users to trade Toncoin directly against Tether on the platform. The listing represents another step in institutional adoption for the Telegram-backed blockchain network.

TON, the native token of the TON blockchain, has seen growing interest from major exchanges. Binance's addition of the USDT pair provides liquidity and direct price discovery against one of crypto's most widely used stablecoins.

The pair joins existing TON trading options on Binance, which already supported spot and futures trading in other pairs. The expansion of trading pairs typically reflects both market demand and the exchange's confidence in an asset's long-term viability.

TON has maintained a position among the top 10 cryptocurrencies by market capitalization, driven by integration with Telegram's ecosystem and growing developer activity on the blockchain. Tether (USDT) remains the dominant stablecoin globally, making USDT pairs a standard gateway for retail and institutional traders entering new assets.

The listing comes as TON continues to expand its presence across major exchanges and defi platforms. No specific announcement was made regarding trading fees, deposit requirements, or promotional incentives tied to the new pair.

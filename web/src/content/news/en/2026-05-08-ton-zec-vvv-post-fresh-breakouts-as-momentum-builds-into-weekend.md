Toncoin, Zcash, and Venice Token have each posted breakouts above critical resistance this week, entering the weekend with expanded trading volume and bullish technical setups, according to analysis circulating among traders on social media.

Toncoin climbed above $2.74 on May 4, breaking a multi-month accumulation zone. The breakout candle registered the largest single-day volume since October, and buy pressure has sustained across subsequent sessions. At current levels, TON trades at the 0.618 Fibonacci retracement—a level derived from the August 2025 high to the April low of $1.12. A daily close above this point would open a path to the 0.786 Fib target near $3.10. On the downside, support sits at the 0.382 Fib around $2.12. Momentum indicators show elevated readings, with the 14-day RSI near 93, though no bearish divergence has formed to suggest an imminent reversal. The timing coincides with fresh institutional attention: Telegram founder Pavel Durov recently announced plans for Telegram itself to become the network's largest validator. Analyst Zach Humphries characterized the move as the beginning of a classic expansion phase, flagging a potential resistance flip at $2.89 that could unlock a longer-term path to $6.

Zcash broke through the 0.618 Fibonacci level at $533 two days ago—a barrier that also aligned with a December 29 swing high. The privacy coin has been trending upward since mid-April. The next upside target sits at the 0.786 Fib near $628. Supportive conditions include a notable institutional position disclosed by Multicoin Capital this week, plus fresh momentum from a Robinhood listing and ongoing ETF speculation. The RSI sits at 86, deep in overbought territory, signaling stretched conditions.

Venice Token, the native asset of the Venice AI ecosystem, has posted the strongest performance. It reached a fresh 2026 high near $13.96, with a Fibonacci extension framework pointing to targets at $17.30 (1.272 extension) and $21.52 (1.618 extension). The 14-day RSI stands at 80 with no bearish divergence visible. All three tokens display momentum stretched to elevated levels, a condition that typically precedes either deeper runs or consolidation breaks.

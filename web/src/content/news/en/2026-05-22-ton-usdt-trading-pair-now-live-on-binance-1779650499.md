Binance, the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by trading volume, has activated trading for TON against USDT on its spot market.

The TON/USDT pair joins an existing selection of TON trading pairs on the platform, giving traders another venue to buy and sell Toncoin at real-time market rates. USDT, the stablecoin issued by Tether, remains one of the most widely used price benchmarks across crypto markets.

The listing represents continued integration of the TON blockchain—backed by Telegram's infrastructure and community—into major centralized trading infrastructure. TON has grown to rank among the top blockchain networks by market capitalization and daily active users.

Binance's decision to expand its TON trading pairs reflects institutional appetite for liquidity around the token. Direct USDT pairs are typically preferred by traders seeking to move capital between TON holdings and dollar-denominated stablecoin reserves without conversion friction.

Exact trading launch time and any promotional incentives have not been detailed in available reporting. Users can access the pair through Binance's standard spot trading interface.

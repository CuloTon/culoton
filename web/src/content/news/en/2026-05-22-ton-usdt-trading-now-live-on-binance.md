Binance has activated trading for the TON/USDT pair, allowing users to exchange Toncoin directly against the stablecoin on the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by volume.

The listing represents continued institutional support for TON, the native token of The Open Network, which launched on Telegram's infrastructure and has grown to become one of the more liquid blockchain assets in the market.

TON/USDT joins existing trading pairs on Binance and enables both spot and margin trading for qualified users. The pair offers direct price discovery between Toncoin and dollar-pegged value, historically a core requirement for establishing stable on-ramp and off-ramp pathways for retail participation.

Binance's decision to list the pair follows broader ecosystem development around TON, including increased developer activity and rising daily active users on the network. The exchange had previously supported TON through other trading pairs and derivatives products.

For traders, the addition of USDT pairing provides lower slippage on significant position sizes compared to indirect routing through other assets. It also streamlines trading mechanics for users who prefer to maintain holdings in stablecoins between trading sessions.

The listing carries no special restrictions or conditions. Standard Binance trading fees and margin requirements apply. The pair is available on both the web platform and mobile application.

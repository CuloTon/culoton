Binance has activated trading for TON against USDT, the largest stablecoin by market capitalisation. The listing provides direct conversion between Toncoin and US Dollar Tether on the exchange's spot market.

The move simplifies trading mechanics for users holding or seeking exposure to Toncoin. Previously, traders typically converted through intermediate pairs or separate transactions. Direct pairings reduce friction and potential slippage in execution.

TON, the native token of the Telegram-backed blockchain, has grown in prominence as the ecosystem expands. Recent months have seen increased developer activity and integration announcements across DeFi protocols and layer-2 solutions built atop the network.

Binance remains the primary exchange listing venue for major blockchain assets. The inclusion of a USDT pair signals confidence in trading volume and user demand, and aligns with the exchange's pattern of supporting established layer-1 networks.

No transaction fees, minimum order sizes, or other trading restrictions were announced alongside the listing. Standard Binance trading rules apply.

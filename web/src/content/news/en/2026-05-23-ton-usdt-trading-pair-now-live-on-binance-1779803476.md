Binance has added TON/USDT to its spot trading offerings, allowing users to trade Toncoin directly against Tether on the world's largest cryptocurrency exchange by volume.

The listing marks another step in TON's integration into major trading venues. Prior to this pair, TON was primarily accessible on Binance through BTC and BUSD pairings, limiting direct stablecoin conversion for some market participants.

TON, the native token of the TON blockchain developed by Telegram, has attracted significant institutional and retail interest since its public launch in 2023. The token powers transaction settlement, validator staking, and smart contract execution across the network.

Direct USDT pairs are typically viewed as a marker of maturity within exchange ecosystems, reducing slippage for larger trades and lowering friction for new market entrants seeking exposure. The addition reflects growing confidence in TON's market depth and demand from Binance's user base.

The move comes as the Telegram ecosystem continues to expand its on-chain footprint. The Open Network has recorded steady growth in active developers and decentralized application launches over the past year.

No specific trading details—minimum order sizes, fee structure, or promotional terms—were disclosed in available exchange announcements. Users can access the pair through Binance's standard spot trading interface.

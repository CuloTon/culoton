Toncoin is treading water. The token held ground near $2 support through the morning as cryptocurrency markets entered a cautious phase, with Bitcoin slipping below $80,000 and triggering a 4.2% decline for TON. Technical analysts are monitoring a potential golden cross—where shorter-term moving averages cross above longer-term ones—as a possible reversal signal, though such patterns carry no guarantees in volatile markets. The broader backdrop matters more than any chart: Bitcoin weakness typically signals risk-off sentiment that suppresses altcoin rallies, though select assets like Dogecoin have bucked the trend.

**On infrastructure,** TON's development team introduced Agentic Wallets, a new standard allowing Telegram-based AI bots to hold and manage cryptocurrency within user-defined spending limits. The framework turns conversational interfaces into direct payment channels—users fund the wallets, bots operate within guardrails, and transactions settle natively in Telegram. For a platform with 900+ million users, many of whom interact daily with bots, the move lowers friction for on-chain adoption and positions TON as a bridge between conversational AI and financial execution.

**On security,** the TAC Protocol bridge exploit has been reclassified as white hat after the attacker returned the majority of $2.8 million in stolen funds. TAC, which bridges TON and Ethereum, took the breach seriously—pausing operations and containing damage to bridged USDT, BLUM, and tsTON tokens. The recovery came swiftly enough that the protocol offered a 10% bounty (roughly 13 ETH plus 300 ZEC) rather than pursue legal action. TAC's token price fell 21% in the interim, though the swift fund recovery and measured response follow standard Web3incident-management practice.

**On adoption,** Belarusian cryptobanks are expanding their offerings to support Toncoin alongside Bitcoin, Tether, and roughly 23 other assets. The move reflects growing institutional appetite for TON exposure, particularly in Eastern Europe where regulatory permissiveness has encouraged blockchain infrastructure development. The selection of 26 coins suggests deliberate curation rather than indiscriminate listing—a strategy that balances market demand against operational complexity.

**Longer-term technical forecasts** put Toncoin at $10-plus by 2029, a 5x stretch from current levels. Such projections rest on the asset's demonstrated volatility—TON has traded as high as $8.24—and the ecosystem's expanding footprint. The token is consolidating around $2.01 with elevated 30-day volatility of 28.87%, leaving near-term direction uncertain pending fresh catalysts or a clearer break in either direction. Watch whether support holds or the $2 floor cracks to deeper levels in coming sessions.
